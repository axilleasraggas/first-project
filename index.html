TRIKALA-METEORA VILLA TOSCANA

Villa Toscana is ideally situated just 10 minutes from Trikala and a convenient 30-minute drive to Meteora. This spacious house is highly recommended for families and large groups, offering ample room to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

The house boasts 4 bedrooms with a total of 12 beds, comfortably accommodating up to 16+ guests with some special arrangements. It offers two bathrooms and a fully equipped kitchen with all your cooking essentials. Additionally, you'll find extra sheets, towels, and pillowcases readily available. Here are some handpicked destinations that are an absolute must-visit on your upcoming trip to Trikala!

The mill of the elves

This charming destination undergoes a magical winter transformation, where the spirit of the season comes alive. As you step in, you'll be greeted by enchanting trees adorned with twinkling lights and festive decorations that'll fill your heart with holiday cheer. T he Mill of the Elves at Christmas isn't just a feast for the eyes; it's a place where the holiday spirit thrives, creating a truly magical experience for everyone who joins in the festivities

Meteora

Meteora, located just 20 minutes away, is renowned for its surreal rock formations that provide a dramatic backdrop to ancient monasteries perched atop them. Built by hermit monks in the 14th century, these architectural marvels embody a rich history of devotion and isolation.Meteora offers a captivating blend of human ingenuity and natural wonder, making it a place of profound cultural and natural significance.

Stone bridge and waterfall of Palaiokarya

Paleokarya, nestled in a ravine of breathtaking natural beauty, is situated 25 kilometers from Trikala city. Just 500 meters before reaching Kato Paleokaria village, you'll encounter the magnificent arched bridge of Paleokaria. This bridge spans 26 meters with a remarkable arch of 19 meters and stands 10 meters above the riverbed. Behind it, a stunning 12-meter waterfall adds to the scenic charm.
